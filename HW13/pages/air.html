'Air' Review: The Game Changers

Written by Alex Convery, “Air” nicely hits the sweet spot between light comedy and lighter drama that's tough to get right. It's funny, but its generous laughs tend to be low-key and are more often dependent on their delivery than on the actual writing. Damon is crucial to selling the humor.
